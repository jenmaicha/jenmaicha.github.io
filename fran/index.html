Happy birthday, Fran!

I hope you'll ejoy this intersection of Shakespeare and Sir Patrick Stewart.

Back in the spring, Sir Patrick undertook to record himself reading one of Shakespeare's sonnets each day, and posted them to Twitter as a kind of free art offering. I've collected them all here for you. Hope this keeps you warm as the days grow short and chilly ❤️
